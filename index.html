01 / About

A short introduction.

Yes, I’m a high school student. exploring aspects of engineering while battling schoolwork, coursework, and a stubborn curiosity for how things actually work under the screen.

I started loving engineering at around 6 years old, when I got my hands on the first computer I've seen and the first real though I had was; "How does this work?" That's basically my life to this day.

My dream has been to pursue this dream career of mine, and I am working hard to make it happen. Sometimes I have to focus on schoolwork, but in the litte gaps, I often find time for projects. I, as many of my peers, worry about the future, whether its college or life after that, but one quote that's helped me:

"We are what we repeatedly do. Excellence, then, is not an act, but a habit".
